Campus Life

Whether it's a snack in Michel Student Center, a concert at Renaissance Coliseum, games with friends at Late Night BU, multicultural activities or a student organization service project, Bradley University’s campus is always busy with fun activities.

Make a Difference

Peoria and the world feel Bradley University’s impact through a variety of short and long-term service opportunities. Students give 50,000 hours each year to nonprofits through weekend community service, alternative spring break trips and projects coordinated through student organizations, Greek life and the Lewis J. Burger Center for Student Leadership and Public Service.
